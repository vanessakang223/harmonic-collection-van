"Symbol"

The word “symbol” comes from the Latin symbolum, that comes from the Greek symbolon, or “sign”. Signs, gestures, objects, animals, stones symbols are a way to comprehend spirituality. We are familiar with symbols, we “live” them, but often we do not really know their true meaning or their origin.
